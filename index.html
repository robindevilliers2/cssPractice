The Problem




Designers and clients do not always have the same understanding

Breakdown in communication.

Keeping track of what has been said to form a design idea.

Design time wasted because of a simple misunderstanding.

The Idea




Give a client a quick test when they start a new project.

Show a designer what the client likes and dislikes.

Avoid unnecessary confusion with a clear image of what the client wants.

Help designers find projects according to their design style

Our Application




A platform for designers and for clients

Clients start a project and takes a quick preference test

Our test tags the project with a style.

A designer can search for a project by style

The designer can assign himself to the project and upload his proposed designs.
